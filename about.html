About Me

I'm a self-taught web developer who wants to know All The Things. I’ve completed every online course offered by the Women’s Coding Collective (who I worked for briefly), and the One Month Ruby on Rails course; and spent a good amount of time learning at Free Code Camp. Most recently I completed the WordPress developer blueprint at Skillcrush.

I have a Bachelor of Arts degree in Classics from Connecticut College, and a Master of Public Administration degree from Suffolk University.

When I'm not writing code or taking care of my two small children, I'm either knitting, thinking about knitting, or drinking coffee.
